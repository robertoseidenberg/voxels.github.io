VOXELS

is a pencil based 3d drawing app for Apple iPad
It is is pretty unfinished and your data is not save

Download at your own risk viaTestflight
Made by@rccbox



Usage notes

Voxels is designed decidedly for use with a pencil.
It's actully impossible to draw without one

Touch interactions are solely used for navigating the menu palettes.
The pencil is used solely for drawing

Drawing is pressure sensitive.
When the pencil rests on the display the insertion point is beeing highlighted

Any drawing operation is beeing performed by applying a bit of pressure.

It works like any other iOS drawing software.
But in 3D.

Panels might be closed by swiping them towards the screen edges using two fingers.
